S.P.Balasubramaniam is one true gem from Indian cine musical field. In a career spanning more than 5 decades, he recorded over a mammoth of 40,000 songs in 16 languages. He had his first national award for the song in critically acclaimed Shankarabharanam film. After that, he received 5 more national awards;two more in Telugu, one in each Tamil, Kannada and Hindi.
